# 🇧🇷 Brazilian E-Commerce Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist**, containing over 100,000 real e-commerce orders from multiple marketplaces in Brazil. The objective is to uncover actionable business insights related to customer behavior, delivery performance, payment preferences, product demand, and emerging trends in AI-driven commerce.

The analysis combines exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical insights, and business intelligence techniques to identify opportunities for improving customer experience, increasing revenue, and optimizing operational efficiency.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### 1. Delivery Performance & Cart Friction

* Majority of orders were delivered before the estimated date.
* Delivery delays significantly impact customer satisfaction.
* High freight costs contribute to cart abandonment.

### 2. Payment Preferences

* Credit cards dominate online transactions.
* Customers using credit cards generally exhibit higher average order values.
* Installment options encourage higher-value purchases.

### 3. Product Category Analysis

* Home essentials and personal care products generate the highest order volumes.
* Premium categories contribute significantly to revenue despite lower sales volumes.
* Category-specific pricing and demand patterns reveal opportunities for targeted marketing.

### 4. AI in E-Commerce

* AI-powered personalization is becoming a major growth driver.
* Product recommendations and intelligent search improve customer engagement.
* Global trends indicate rapid adoption of AI technologies in digital commerce.

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

### Logistics Optimization

* Reduce delayed deliveries through better logistics planning.
* Improve inventory management for high-demand products.

### Payment Strategy

* Promote installment-based credit card payments.
* Expand Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) options.

### Category Growth

* Cross-sell premium products with high-volume categories.
* Develop targeted marketing campaigns for top-performing categories.

### AI Adoption

* Invest in recommendation systems.
* Enhance product discovery using AI-powered search.
* Improve customer support through intelligent automation.
